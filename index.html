What we provide

     

Comprehensive services designed to keep your mine running with predictable costs and maximum safety.

     
       
         

Equipment Provisioning

         

Range: 50–350 tonne haul trucks, hydraulic shovels, excavators, dozers, drill rigs, graders, water trucks and conveyors. All equipment is inspected and certified before deployment.

       
       
         

Ops & Crew Outsourcing

         

Supply of certified operators, shift management, fatigue monitoring and site supervision. Local-hire programs built into each scope.

       
       
         

Preventive & Predictive Maintenance

         

Condition-based monitoring (telemetry), OEM-aligned maintenance schedules and guaranteed response times for critical repairs.

       
       
         

Spare Parts & Logistics

About GraniteCore

     

Founded in 2013 by mining and engineering professionals, GraniteCore Heavy Services was established to provide a commercially attractive alternative to heavy equipment ownership. We combine OEM relationships, on-ground logistics and a safety-centric operations culture to deliver measurable results.

     
       
         

Mission

         

To enable mining operations to scale safely and efficiently by providing reliable, fully-managed heavy equipment solutions.

       
       
         

Vision

         

A mining industry where gear availability and safety are predictable — so operators can focus on production excellence.

       
       
         

Values

         

Safety first • Reliability • Transparency • Local empowerment • Sustainability
